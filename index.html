About Nuelook

Welcome to Nuelook!

Nuelook is an innovative beauty-reseller concept founded in Malaysia by Abdul Hadi in 2024, designed to cater to the modern skincare needs of youngsters and skincare enthusiasts. Specializing in skincare, Nuelook’s open-sell environment offers a carefully curated selection of renowned and emerging brands, along with its own trend-driven private label products tailored to meet diverse skin goals.

We are dedicated to creating a dynamic, customer-centric experience with personalized guidance, sustainable practices, and community engagement to foster long-lasting relationships with our customers.
